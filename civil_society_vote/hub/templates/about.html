{% trans "About RoHelp" %}

{% trans "Right now we are in the midst of what is probably the biggest crisis of our lifetime, a crisis that we are all vulnerable to, regardless of where we are or our social context, a crisis whose effects will be felt for many months to come. Fortunately the NGO sector is on the front lines of the battle against the effects of the epidemic; from covering gaps in the health system to creating or supplementing response capacity for the public sector or offering support to those most vulnerable, Romanian NGOs, both small and large, have been vital in protecting each and everyone of us." %}

{% trans "Despite consistent support from the business sector, NGO efforts that we depend upon can't go on as is in the months to come and can become sustainable only if we too contribute." %}

{% trans "Code for Romania, through its volunteer community, had developed RoHelp.ro, a complete digital platform offered to NGOs actively involved in limiting the effects of the COVID-19 epidemic, by helping collect the resources they dearly need. By integrating the Netopia payment processor with the platform, Rohelp.ro helps participating eligible organizations directly collect the resources they need, with no hidden fees or middlemen. NGOs can also request and receive material or volunteer resources directly through the platform. The needs of every organization on the platform can be seen according to how stringent the need is and where the organization is located." %}

{% trans "RoHelp.ro is a platform built with the support of the Romanian American Foundation, ING Bank and The Vodafone Foundation Romania in partnership with the Romanian Government via the Authority for the Digitalization of Romania, the Emergency Services Department and the Romanian Community Foundations Federation. It is open to all associations and foundations legally registered in Romania that are implementing measures to help ease the COVID-19 crisis and are not political in nature nor discriminate on the basis of race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, physical or mental ability or social/economic class." %}

{% trans "To ensure that every donation is accounted for and used for its intended purpose Code for Romania in partnership with the Romanian Community Foundations Federation has developed a clear set of rules and a transparent validation and reporting system, that will help us ensure that the platform is administered to the benefit of all." %}

{% trans "How RoHelp got started" %}

{% blocktrans with link="https://civiclabs.ro/ro/domains/pregatire-si-raspuns-in-situatii-de-urgenta" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" %} “There is no uniform distribution of resources” is the 19th issue identified in Code for Romania’s own 2019-2020 Civic Labs year 1 research paper in the field of Emergency Preparedness and Disaster Management in the case of a major earthquake. In brief, starting from the analysis of the response in the case of Mexico or Italy, two countries that have had to manage response to a major earthquake recently (2017 and 2016, respectively) we realized that any fundraising effort during or after a major disaster comes together with a multitude of risks, including the sudden appearance of phantom organizations or focusing aid to just a few areas of intervention,, either thematic or geographical, according to the communication capacity of NGOs in the field. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans with link="https://civiclabs.ro/ro/solutions/aid-management-hub" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" %} The Code for Romania COVID-19 Task Force used the Civic Labs research program results in the field of emergency response, and adapted our Aid Management Hub prototype to the present situation, developing RoHelp.ro in record time. {% endblocktrans %}


{% trans "RoHelp is a project done through the efforts of the Code for Romania volunteers and team. Code for Romania is an independent, unaffiliated and apolitical NGO. This project is developed as part of the Code for Romania Task Force, in partnership with the Romanian Community Foundations Federation, the Romanian Government via the Authority for the Digitalization of Romania and the Emergency Services Department." %}
