{% trans "General Rules for associations and foundations accessing the Platform" %}

{% trans "RoHelp is a relief management platform developed by the Code for Romania Association in partnership with the Romanian Government through the Authority for the Digitalization of Romania and administered by Code for Romania." %}

{% trans "RoHelp centralizes all the associations and foundations that are actively working to ameliorate the crisis generated by the COVID-19 epidemic by offering them a platform through which they can collect all the necessary financial and material resources necessary in their activities (e.g. monney, material aid, volunteer labor etc)." %}

{% trans "Financial Resources are collected by the associations and foundations that have registered an account with the Platform directly, through their allotted account, on the basis of a contract signed with the payment operator Netopia." %}

{% trans "The following types of entities are eligible for enrollment on the RoHelp Platform:" %}

  • {% blocktrans %} active associations and foundations that are directly implementing support programs in response to the COVID-19 crisis {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %} Associations and foundations that collect financial or material resources in order to distribute them towards host organizations or other compatible initiatives (grant managers) {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %} The Red Cross {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} All associations and foundations that will use the RoHelp Platform for collecting financial or material resources have to explicitly declare the destination for said resources as well as the way they will be used. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "In order to enroll in and use the RoHelp Platform, associations and foundations have to submit a written request form, a request that will be analysed by a committee in order to establish if they are eligible." %}

{% trans "The written request must include a description of the main activities that the association or foundation has been undergoing, with a focus on any programs or actions taken in the context of the crisis caused by the COVID-19 epidemic. The association or foundation must also include in the written request the types of resources that it anticipates as necessary for combatting the effects if the current epidemic. The request must be submitted along with electronic copies of the following documents: the first page of the last organizational yearly balance sheet (with the exception of organizations founded in the last 12 months), the statute of the association or foundation, with any confidential data blocked out as well as documents verifying that the asociation is in the national Associations and Foundations Registry." %}

{% trans "The eligibility criteria for enrolling in the platform are the following:" %}

  • {% trans "Filling out the enrollment form (in Romanian)," %}
  • {% trans "Submitting all required paperwork (see above)" %}
  • {% trans "The enrolling organization is involved in actions to solve or ameliorate the effects gof the COVID-19 epidemic crisis," %}
  • {% trans "The enrolling organization is not involved in actions nor does it support causes of a political nature or that discriminate on the basis of ethnic background, race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, physical or mental ability or social/economic class, and the management of the organisation is not politically involved" %}

{% trans "An applicant’s eligibility will be determined by a committee formed by the following three members: a Code for Romania Representative, an Emergency Services Department (DSU) representative and a representative of the Community Foundations of Romania Federation." %}

{% trans "The committee members will ensure that there is no conflict of interest to hinder them from participating in this evaluatory capacity and, therefore, have no direct or indirect interes of a financial, personal or any other nature that would prevent them from being impartial and independent." %}

{% trans "The committee will analyse the documents submitted by applicants and can ask for further documents or information. The decision regarding the applicant’s eligibility will be relayed to the applicant with a full explanation of the motives for said decision." %}

{% trans "If an applicant is refused access to the Platform they have the right to ask, a single time, for a re-evaluation, by transmitting setra documents and information." %}

{% static "data/acord_de_parteneriat.pdf" as the_url %} {% blocktrans with target="_blank" %} Any applicant deemed eligible will sign a Partnership Agreement with Code for Romania, agreement that will detail each party’s rights and obligations regarding the use of the RoHelp Platform, including the obligation of the association or foundation to declare and respect the destination of the material and financial resources collected through the Platform and the obligation to report how said resources are being used. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "The reporting regarding the usage of resources collected through the Platform must be done after the first month of Platform use and every quarter afterwards, as well as at the end of collaboration. The reports will be analysed and validated by a committee with a similar makeup to the one validating the enrollments. The reporting will be done directly, via the platform, with each organization having the obligation to give out all requested information." %}
